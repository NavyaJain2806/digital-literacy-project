# Stay Safe Online — Prevention Checklist

---

## General Online Safety

- [ ] Use strong passwords with at least 12 characters — mix uppercase,
      lowercase, numbers and symbols. Never reuse the same password
      across multiple accounts.

- [ ] Enable Two-Factor Authentication (2FA) on all important accounts
      including Gmail, GitHub, LinkedIn and college portals.

- [ ] Never click on links received from unknown senders via email,
      WhatsApp, or social media — verify the source before opening.

- [ ] Review app permissions before installing any application on your
      phone. Deny access to camera, microphone or contacts unless
      absolutely necessary.

- [ ] Avoid using public Wi-Fi for logging into college portals, email,
      or any sensitive accounts. Use mobile data or a VPN instead.

- [ ] Keep your phone, browser and all apps updated regularly — updates
      patch security vulnerabilities that hackers actively exploit.

- [ ] Log out of your accounts on shared or public devices such as
      college computer labs immediately after use.

- [ ] Think before you post — anything shared online is permanent and
      can be screenshotted even after deletion.

---

## Financial & UPI Safety (India-Specific)

- [ ] Never share your UPI PIN, OTP, or bank account details with
      anyone — not even someone claiming to be from your bank or
      government. No legitimate organisation will ever ask for these.

- [ ] Be alert to fake UPI payment requests — scammers send money
      requests disguised as payment notifications. Always check whether
      you are approving a payment OUT or receiving money IN before
      entering your PIN.

---

## If Something Goes Wrong

- [ ] Report any cybercrime or financial fraud immediately — do not
      wait, as early reporting increases the chance of recovery.
