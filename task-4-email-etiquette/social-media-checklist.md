# Social Media Do's and Don'ts Checklist

| ✅ DO'S | ❌ DON'TS |
|--------|---------|
| Keep your profiles professional and up-to-date. | Don't post offensive, discriminatory, or hateful content. |
| Think before you post — ask yourself if you'd be comfortable with your professor seeing it. | Don't share personal details like your phone number, address, or ID publicly. |
| Use privacy settings to control who sees your content. | Don't engage in or encourage cyberbullying or online harassment. |
| Give credit when sharing others' work or ideas. | Don't post confidential or sensitive information about your institution or classmates. |
| Report harmful or abusive content using platform tools. | Don't use social media to spread unverified news or misinformation. |
