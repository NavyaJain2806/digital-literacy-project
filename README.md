## Project Overview

This repository is the complete submission for the CSE0001 Digital Literacy
project built around the role of a **Student Digital
Ambassador** — a student responsible for helping peers navigate the digital
world safely and professionally.

The portfolio is titled **"Digital Edge: A Student's Guide to Smart & Safe
Online Living"** and is structured across five tasks, each mapped to one
module of the course.

In Task 1, a one-page Digital Literacy Awareness Infographic was designed
using Canva, covering what digital literacy means, useful tools for students,
and safe internet practices. In Task 2, professional accounts were created on
GitHub, LinkedIn, and Kaggle — forming the foundation of a long-term digital
portfolio. In Task 3, the 'Solve Me First' challenge was completed on
HackerRank, and a five-question Digital Literacy Awareness Quiz was built
using Google Forms. In Task 4, two professional emails were drafted — one
requesting an assignment extension and one expressing internship interest —
along with a Social Media Do's and Don'ts checklist. In Task 5, a case study
and prevention checklist were produced on the topic of Cyberbullying,
including real-world statistics and actionable safety tips for college students.

## Module Summaries

### Module 1 – Presentation Software & Spreadsheets
Covers the basics of creating presentations using tools like Microsoft PowerPoint, Prezi, and Canva. Also includes fundamentals of electronic spreadsheets — functions, formulas, charts, and graphics in Microsoft Excel.

### Module 2 – Programming & Collaborative Learning Platforms
Introduction to collaborative tools like Google Docs, Slides, Sheets, and Forms. Coding practice platforms covered include HackerRank, HackerEarth, CodeChef, and CodeTantra, along with virtual laboratories.

### Module 3 – Building a Digital Portfolio
Focuses on creating a professional online presence using platforms such as LinkedIn, GitHub, Kaggle, ResearchGate, Stack Overflow, TechGig, and others. This module emphasizes long-term career visibility.

### Module 4 – Email Etiquette & Social Media Etiquette
Covers the importance of professional email communication — subject lines, greetings, tone, font choice, signatures, attaching files, and replying correctly. Also introduces the basics of responsible social media use.

### Module 5 – Cybercrime & Financial Fraud Awareness
Describes types of cybercrimes including phishing, identity theft, UPI fraud, and cyberbullying. Includes prevention measures, digital hygiene practices, social media etiquette, and online certification platforms.
