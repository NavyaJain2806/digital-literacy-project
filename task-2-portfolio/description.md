## Reflection

For Task 2, I set up accounts on three platforms: GitHub, LinkedIn, and
Kaggle. GitHub is the most important platform for a Computer Science
student — it acts as a living portfolio of all coding work and projects.
I created a profile README repository with my name, branch, year, and
what I hope to learn during my degree.

LinkedIn is the largest professional networking platform globally, used
by employers, internship coordinators, and academic researchers. I
filled in my Education section with my B.Tech details, VIT Bhopal
University, and my expected graduation year.

Kaggle is a platform specialising in data science and machine learning,
offering datasets, competitions, and courses. Creating an account here
reflects my interest in data-related fields as a CSE student.
